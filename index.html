One local community, two mesh platforms.

OmaMesh is a grassroots group of Omaha-area radio enthusiasts exploring long-range, low-power mesh networking powered by both Meshtastic and MeshCore systems.

What we’re working on

Two platforms, one social layer

The community is human, not brand-specific. Whether you’re curious about Meshtastic, MeshCore, or just learning what a mesh network is, you’ll find people comparing notes and testing ideas together.

MeshCore Settings

Our group has found Spreading Factor 9 (SF9) works better than the default SF7. Use the USA/Canada (Recommended) preset, change Spreading Factor to 9, and if you run a repeater, set Coding Rate to 8 before rebooting.
